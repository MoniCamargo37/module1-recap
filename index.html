GAME BEGIN

Outward is a Western RPG with a Wide-Open Sandbox world. It is set in a Low Fantasy realm of Aurai, a setting that can be described as a cross between Dark Souls and Kingdom Come: Deliverance, with its combat system and world-building being largely inspired by the former, and possessing the same sort of Survival Sandbox elements as the latter. It can also be played alone, or in co-op.
